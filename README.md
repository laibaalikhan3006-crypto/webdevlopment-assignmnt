            <h1>FETTUCCINE PASTA </h1>
            <h2>Recipe </h2>
        
            <img src="https://www.sharmispassions.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/12/alfredopasta5-500x500.jpg" alt=""FETTUCCINE PASTA width="200 ",height="300" >
        <h2>Ingredients</h2>
        <ol> 
            <li>–Butter 2 tbsp</li> 
            <li>– Flour (maida) 2 tbsp</li>
            <li>– 2 cups Milk</li> 
            <li>– Salt-to taste </li>  
            <li>– Black pepper ½ tsp</li>
            <li>– Optional : garlic, cheese</li> 
        </ol>
        <h2>STEPS TO FOLLOW </h2>
        <p> Melt butter in a pan on medium heat <br>Add flour and cook for 1–2 minutes <b>(don’t brown it)</b> <br> Slowly add milk while stirring continuously <b>(no lumps!)</b> <br>Keep stirring until it becomes smooth and thick 
            <br> Add salt and black pepper <br><b>Optional: <i>Add garlic or cheese for extra flavor</i></b></p>
